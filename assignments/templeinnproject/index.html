Featured Services

Our Family History Research lab provides guests with computers, free wifi, and family history consultants on site. Available as a special service for all guests.

Free breakfast services are included with every room and suite. We have a wide range of healthy breakfast options available, including gluten, dairy, and sugar free. Let your Temple Inn & Suites representative know if you or anyone in your party have any dietary restrictions so that we can accommodate you.

Temple Inn & Suites provides free of charge special changing rooms and wash services for guests who may have travelled a long distance and need to freshen up before entering the House of the Lord.
